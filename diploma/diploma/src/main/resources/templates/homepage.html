The objective of this project:

The objective of this project is to develop a Web application that allows students to browse available diploma thesis projects from various professors and apply for the diploma thesis projects that interest them. The application further allows professors to assign diploma thesis projects to students, supervise the assigned theses projects and assess the outcomes.
